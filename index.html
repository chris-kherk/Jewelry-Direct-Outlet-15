Myths About Moissanite Jewelry Debunked

Moissanite has been gaining popularity as a stunning alternative to diamonds in recent years. However, with its rise in prominence, several myths and misconceptions have also emerged. In this article, we'll debunk the top 5 myths about moissanite jewelry and reveal why it's a valuable choice for discerning jewelry lovers.

Myth 1: Moissanite is Just a Cheap Diamond Imitation

Moissanite is a naturally occurring mineral, silicon carbide, first discovered in a meteor crater. While it's now primarily lab-created for jewelry, moissanite has its own set of impressive characteristics:

  • Brilliance refractive index of 2.65-2.69, higher than a diamond's 2.42
  • Offers more fire and sparkle than diamonds
  • Ranks 9.25 on the Mohs scale of hardness (diamonds are 10)

Myth 2: Moissanite Looks Fake or Plastic

Reality: High-quality moissanite can be virtually indistinguishable from diamonds to the naked eye.

Modern moissanite production techniques have resulted in stones that exhibit:

  • Exceptional clarity
  • Colorless or near-colorless appearance (D-F color range)
  • Brilliant light performance

Many jewelers and even some gemologists have difficulty telling moissanite from diamond without specialized equipment. Take a closer look at our moissanite necklaces to appreciate their beauty.

Myth 3: Moissanite Will Cloud or Change Color Over Time

Reality: Moissanite is extremely durable and stable, maintaining its clarity and color indefinitely.

Unlike some other diamond alternatives:

  • Moissanite doesn't cloud, discolor, or degrade over time
  • It's resistant to scratching, chipping, and breaking
  • The stone can withstand high temperatures and chemical exposure

For jewelry that lasts a lifetime, explore our moissanite earrings collection.

Myth 4: Moissanite Has No Resale Value

Reality: While not as established as the diamond market, moissanite does have resale value.

Factors influencing moissanite's resale value include:

  • Growing popularity and acceptance in the jewelry market
  • Consistent quality due to lab creation
  • Ethical and environmental benefits appealing to conscious consumers

Myth 5: Moissanite is Only for Engagement Rings

Reality: Moissanite is versatile and suitable for all types of jewelry.

Moissanite's durability and brilliance make it ideal for:

  • Earrings
  • Pendants
  • Bracelets
  • Men's jewelry
  • Fashion rings

Attribute Moissanite Diamond

Fire More (0.104 dispersion) Less (0.044 dispersion)

Moissanite offers a brilliant, durable, and ethical alternative to diamonds. By understanding the truth behind these common myths, you can make an informed decision about whether moissanite is right for you.
